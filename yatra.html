💡 Travel Wisdom

Yatra Tips

📅
Best Time to Visit
Oct–March is ideal for most temples. Kedarnath is open May–Nov only. Avoid peak summer for South India temples.
🚂
Book Trains Early
Book IRCTC trains 60–90 days in advance, especially for Tatkal. Rajdhani and Duronto are most comfortable.
🙏
VIP Darshan
Most temples offer VIP/paid darshan. Book online for Tirupati, Kedarnath, Vaishno Devi to skip long queues.
🌿
Pack Light & Sacred
Carry prasad, yellow/white/orange clothes, chunni for ladies. Leave leather and non-veg food at home.
💊
Health & Altitude
For Kedarnath/Char Dham, acclimatize for 2 days. Carry Diamox, warm clothes and basic medicines.
📱
Offline Maps
Download Google Maps offline for Uttarakhand — network is unreliable in the Himalayas.
