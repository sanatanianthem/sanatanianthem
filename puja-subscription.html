Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my puja video? +
After each puja, the purohit uploads the video to Google Drive. Within 2–4 hours, you'll receive an email with the video link. The video is yours to keep and watch anytime.
Can I change my temple after subscribing? +
Yes! You can change your temple once per billing cycle. Simply email us or use the customer portal. Changes take effect from the next month.
What type of puja is performed? +
Our purohits perform Satyanarayan Puja, Abhishek, Aarti, or the temple's primary daily puja — based on what's most auspicious at that temple. Your name and gotra are specifically mentioned during the sankalp (holy intent-setting).
Is ₹200 charged every month automatically? +
Yes, ₹200 is automatically charged on the same date every month via Razorpay. You'll get a payment receipt by email. If payment fails, we'll notify you and pause your subscription temporarily.
What if a puja is missed due to temple closure? +
If a temple is closed on your scheduled date (holidays, special events), we reschedule the puja to the nearest available date and notify you in advance.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
You can cancel anytime by emailing us at [email protected] or via the cancellation link in any of our emails. Your subscription will end at the current month's close with no further charges.
